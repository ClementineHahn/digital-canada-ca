Interested in joining CDS?

Our mission is to build government services that are simple and easy to use. We work with federal departments to focus on users: the millions of Canadians and people around the world who use government services every day. We deploy small, interdisciplinary teams of developers, designers and UX researchers, data scientists, and product managers to work with departments, using agile methods to prototype, build, and deploy digital service solutions. We are building a team of talented, passionate, and driven people who will help make it happen. If you are ready to work on some of Canada’s biggest service delivery opportunities, like improving services for immigrants and veterans, join us for the opportunity to make a real impact for your country.

We're also looking for a CEO for the Canadian Digital Service! We're currently recruiting an experienced and inspiring Chief Executive to lead the CDS team, in executing a bold agenda with a lasting impact. Find out more about the role and how to apply.

The job details

We are hiring in five streams. We have positions available at a variety of levels and durations, depending on the position and need. We are currently hiring in Ottawa, but we plan to eventually have teams or individuals working across the country. This is a rolling campaign, so you can apply any time. We are building a team that is diverse in gender, indigenous heritage, race, disability, age, language, and professional background.

What we're looking for

You:

  • Are passionate about user experience, and want to create measurable improvements to service delivery.

  • Love working in interdisciplinary teams and with external partners.

  • Create structure out of ambiguity, and forge a path in new environments.

  • Are excited about working in the open to benefit a bigger community, and sharing success and failures.

  • Are curious, and your humility helps you learn and grow.

  • Are in love with the problem, not the solution.

Mandatory requirements:

  • Candidates must be fluent in one of Canada’s two official languages, English or French.

  • Preference will be given to Canadian citizens or permanent residents.

  • All positions require the ability to obtain a Government of Canada security clearance at either the Reliability or Secret level depending on the position (we will help you with this if you are offered a position).

Streams

Data

  • You use the tools of your trade easily, such as R, Python, GraphQL, and big data technologies such as Hadoop.

  • Use data to share insights, tell a story, and help your team and clients make decisions.

  • You have solved wicked problems through your in-depth knowledge and work in data analytics, performance measurement, or data visualization.

  • You want to work with your team and clients to connect the right solutions to the unique needs of the projects.

Design

  • Have built an extensive portfolio of design experience by working in experience design, interaction design, or user research.

  • Have a track record of removing complexity from services or products, and you champion simplicity.

  • Are great at communicating with people who are not familiar with design, comfortable working directly with clients during the design process, and are excited to work with people on user research and testing.

  • Take an approach that is inclusive and accessible from the start.

  • Are able to sketch and prototype your ideas on paper or in code.

Developer

  • Have a passion and skill for delivering high quality code, in languages such as Javascript, PHP, Ruby, Python, SASS, and you have worked with technologies like NodeJS, Rails, React, Angular, Serverless.

  • Are comfortable building APIs in a RESTful style or with GraphQL.

  • Are used to working on an agile team, in sprints, constantly iterating, and are excited about Test-Driven Development.

  • May contribute to open source projects and share your work on GitHub or other public code repositories.

Engagement

  • You have experience in building an impressive public communications presence, with your background in one or more of these areas: content writing, stakeholder engagement, or training and learning.

  • You readily learn about new areas, and through your communication help others learn and engage with new ideas.

  • You, ideally, are fluent in both English and French.

  • You are a communicator who has experience working in diverse mediums, such as writing, video production, social media and graphic design.

Product Manager

  • Have significant leadership experience and a proven track-record of building and delivering high quality digital products or services, including evolving the vision for a product and managing its development from start to finish.

  • Can multitask on multiple products while working in an agile environment, and work across the full spectrum of your team to remove roadblocks during the product journey.

  • Are equally comfortable working with users, clients, development teams, and senior management.
