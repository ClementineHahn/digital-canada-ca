Join CDS

We’re building a team of talented, passionate, and driven people to work on some of Canada’s biggest service delivery opportunities. From improving the citizenship process to helping veterans access their benefits, this is an opportunity to make a real impact for your country. Learn more about our mission.

Our small, interdisciplinary teams are made up of developers, designers, researchers, data scientists, and product managers. They work with departments in an agile fashion to prototype, build, and deploy digital service solutions. Learn more about how we work.

The job details

We're hiring in five streams:

  • Communications and Capacity Building
  • Data
  • Design
  • Development
  • Product management

We’re also recruiting an experienced Chief Executive Office (CEO). Learn more about the CEO role and how to apply.

Our hiring is a rolling campaign, so you can apply any time.

We’re looking for candidates of various levels of experience and are happy to offer different durations of employment, depending on what's needed. While we're currently hiring in Ottawa, we also plan to have teams or individuals working across the country. We’re building a team that’s diverse in gender, indigenous heritage, race, disability, age, language, and professional background.

What we're looking for

You:

  • Are passionate about a user’s experience and want to create measurable improvements to service delivery.

  • Love working in interdisciplinary teams and with external partners.

  • Create structure out of ambiguity, and forge a path in new environments.

  • Are excited about working in the open to benefit a larger community, and about sharing success and failures.

  • Are curious, and your humility helps you learn and grow.

  • Are in love with the problem, not the solution.

Mandatory requirements:

  • Candidates must be fluent in one of Canada’s two official languages, English or French.

  • Preference will be given to Canadian citizens or permanent residents.

  • All candidates must be able to obtain a Government of Canada security clearance at either the Reliability or Secret level. We'll help you with this if you're offered a position.

Streams

Communications and Capacity Building

  • You have experience in building an impressive public communications presence. Your background is in one or more of these areas: content writing, stakeholder engagement, or training and learning.

  • You readily learn about new areas and, through your communication, help others learn and engage with new ideas.

  • You're fluent in both English and French.

  • You are a communicator who's experienced in diverse mediums, such as writing, video production, social media and graphic design.

Data

Engineers, Analysts and Scientists

  • You use the tools of your trade easily, such as Python, Scala, Java, Ruby, Go, .NET and big data technologies such as Hadoop.

  • Use data to share insights, tell a story, and help your team and clients make decisions.

  • You've solved wicked problems through your in-depth knowledge and work in data analytics, performance measurement, or data visualization.

  • You want to work with your team and clients to connect the right solutions to the unique needs of the projects.

Design

User experience, interaction design, service design & design research

  • You have an extensive portfolio in one of the above areas of design.

  • You have a track record of removing complexity from services or products, and you champion simplicity.

  • You’re great at communicating with people who aren’t familiar with design, comfortable working alongside clients during the design process, and are excited to work with people on research and testing.

  • You take an approach that is inclusive and accessible from the start.

  • You’re able to sketch and prototype your ideas on paper, in software or code.

Development

Front-end, back-end, dev ops

  • You have a passion and skill for delivering high quality code, in languages such as Javascript, PHP, Ruby, Python, SASS, and you’ve worked with technologies like NodeJS, Rails, React, Angular, Serverless.

  • You’re comfortable building APIs in a RESTful style or with GraphQL.

  • You’re used to working on an agile team, in sprints, constantly iterating, and are excited about Test-Driven Development.

  • You might also contribute to open source projects and share your work on GitHub or other public code repositories.

Product Management

  • You have significant leadership experience and a proven track-record of building and delivering high quality digital products or services, including evolving the vision for a product and managing its development from start to finish.

  • You can multitask on multiple products while working in an agile environment, and work across the full spectrum of your team to remove roadblocks during the product journey.

  • You're as comfortable working with users and clients as you are with development teams and senior management.
